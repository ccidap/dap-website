Who we are

a group of students dedicated to supporting, promoting, and representing the College of Computing and Informatics at UNC Charlotte

Our mission

to create a better environment within CCI, by increasing diversity, promoting engagement, and fostering community and inclusiveness

What we do

Coffee Hours

A weekly student engagment event with themes like social hour, coffee with professors and guest speakers from the industry

Humans of CCI Spotlight

A spotlight on our faculty and students who are doing great things

CCI Picture Day

An opportunity for students to have professional photos taken of themselves to better their personal brand

Student Engagement Rewards Program (SERP)

rewards students for their engagement by assigning points for events attended within CCI

CCI Guidebook

An application which serves as the hub for information about CCI, student leaders, organizations and more

Highschool and Community College Outreach

A targeted recruiting approach for high schools in the Charlotte/Mecklenburg region which focuses on women in computing and unreached fields like digital art and math
