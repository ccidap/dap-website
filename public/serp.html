Student Engagement Rewards Program

What is SERP?

A community effort to engage students within the College of Computing and Informatics and reward those who are actively engaged year round.

How does it work?

With your UNCC 49er ID card! Simply head to a CCI sponsored event and a CCI event organizer will swipe your ID card. For each event you attend you gain a point. At the end of the year, the students with the most points will receive prizes. Events are listed on Be The Future of Computing in the Guidebook App

General Guidelines

Events must be open to all members of the organization

Students must participate for at least 25% of the meeting

Student organizations must swipe in all attendees
